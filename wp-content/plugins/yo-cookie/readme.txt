== Description ==   

Cookie - flexible plugins to show and manage cookie and privacy agreement. Yo Cookie is fully GDPR compatible. With Yo Cookie you can simple configure cookie message dialog to make visitor agree with your policy.
Panel of the cooke dialog is fully customizable. You can change colors, styles of the privacy link, cookies text and agree button. You can also customize position and size of the Yo Cookie panel. 
This plugin help you to make sure that all cookie compliance terms will be followed. Make sure that your website follow all terms of the coookie law and gdpr requriements. 

= Key Features of Cookie Panel =
*  Simple configuraton settings of the cookie panel interface styles;
*  Customizable cookie agree buttons;
*  Simple color selector of the cookie terms panel text;
*  Color selector for the cookie panel background color;
*  Easy color pick up for the cookie panel border;
*  Wide range of the cookie panel positions defined in cookie settings;
*  Customizable cookie agree button title;
*  Customizable padding for the cookie panel position;
*  Cookie panel transporancy customization in admin section;
*  Editable cookie policy text with HTML tags support;
*  Do not limited type of the content in cookie panel;
